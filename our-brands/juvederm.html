JUVÉDERM®

Hyaluronic acid + Lidocaine Hydrochloride

Juvéderm is a brand of hyaluronic acid Facial Fillers1*: Our full range of hyaluronic acid (HA) facial fillers is built on Allergan Aesthetics heritage of quality, science and meaningful innovation. Each gel is carefully designed with specific properties to address different areas of the face¶.

Following Juvéderm range of products are approved in India

Juvéderm® Ultra Plus XC

Hyaluronic Acid 24 mg + Lidocaine Hydrochloride 3mg

Indications: It is intended for filling mid and/or deep depression of the skin via mid and/or deep dermis injection, and lip definition and enhancement. The presence of lidocaine is meant to reduce the patient’s pain during treatment2.

Juvéderm® Voluma with Lidocaine

Hyaluronic Acid 20 mg + Lidocaine Hydrochloride 3mg

Indications: It is an injectable implant indicated to restore volume of the face by injection by an authorized medical practitioner in accordance with local applicable regulation. The presence of lidocaine is meant to reduce the pain during the treatment3.

Juvéderm® Volift with Lidocaine

Hyaluronic Acid 17.5mg + Lidocaine Hydrochloride 3mg

Indications: Treatment of deep skin depressions, face contouring and volume restoration via deep dermis injection as well as for the restoration of lip volume by an authorized medical practitioner in accordance with local applicable regulations. The presence of lidocaine is meant to reduce the patient’s pain during treatment5.

Juvéderm® Volbella with Lidocaine

Hyaluronic Acid 15mg + Lidocaine Hydrochloride 3mg

Indications: treatment of fine lines and medium sized skin depressions such as peri-oral, peri orbital, glabellar, neck and forehead lines by injection in the superficial/mid-dermis. Juvéderm® VOLBELLA™ with Lidocaine can be used to correct infraorbital skin depressions (tear trough) via deep (submuscular / pre periosteal) injection. Juvéderm® VOLBELLA™ with Lidocaine should be injected by a trained and registered medical practitioner. The presence of lidocaine is meant to reduce the patient’s pain during treatment6.

Juvéderm® Volux

Hyaluronic Acid 25mg + Lidocaine Hydrochloride 3 mg

Indications: Juvéderm® VOLUX is an injectable implant intended to restore and create volume of the face. The presence of lidocaine is meant to reduce the patient’s pain during treatment7.
