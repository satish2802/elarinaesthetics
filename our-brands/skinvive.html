SKINVIVE™

SKINVIVE™ is an injectable hyaluronic acid used for improving skin quality attributes such as hydration and elasticity. SKINVIVE™ hydrates‖ and refreshes¶ from within1,2,3,** for radiant,†† smooth‡‡ and healthy-looking* skin.1

For more information about SKINVIVE™ in India, visit us at:

skinvive.co.in

Footnotes

† Skin hydration was measured using the MoistureMeterD instrument with the XS 5 and S 15 probes (depth of effective measurement: 0.5 and 1.5 mm, respectively).2

‡ Long lasting is up to 9 months following a single treatment.2

§ Instrument measures of skin hydration, smoothness, and skin deformation parameters were performed on the cheek, forehead, and neck of one side of the face on Day 0 and at 30 days after initial treatment (before touch up), Months 1, 4, 6, and 9 after the last treatment, and Month 1R. Skin hydration was measured using the MoistureMeterD instrument (n=131) and top-up treatment administered at Day 30 (n=31).2

‖‖ Based on a search of published clinical studies of injectable HA treatments in March 2023. A duration of 9 months was established with SKINVIVE™.4

¶ Lasting up to 9 months as reported by 51.2% of patients. As reported by 127 of 131 patients (2 subjects did not respond) in a composite satisfaction score, which includes how refreshed their facial skin looked.1

** Data derived from a prospective, non-randomised, interventional, open-label study where 11 healthy patients (men [n=2] and women [n=9]) received intradermal VYC-12L treatment on the volar forearm. Clinical probes assessed skin quality at baseline and Months 1 and 3 post-treatment. Punch biopsies were collected at Months 1 and 3 posttreatment to evaluate histologic and genomic changes.3

†† Lasting up to 9 months as reported by 63% of patients. As reported by 127 of 131 patients in a composite satisfaction score, which includes how radiant their facial skin looked.1

‡‡ Lasting up to 9 months as reported by 55.1% of patients. As reported by 127 of 131 patients in a composite satisfaction score, which includes how smooth their facial skin looked.1

* Lasting up to 9 months as reported by 76.2% of patients. As reported by 127 of 131 patients (1 subject did not respond) in a composite satisfaction score, which includes how healthy their skin looked.1
